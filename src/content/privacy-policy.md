## Introduction

Texas Covenant Home Inspections ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and how we use it.

## Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect:

- Your name
- Your email address
- Your phone number, if you choose to provide it
- The contents of your message

We use Google Ads conversion tracking to measure whether visitors who find us through our advertising complete meaningful actions on this website, such as submitting the contact form. Google may set cookies or use similar technologies as part of this service. Learn more in [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit only to:

- Read and respond to your inquiry
- Schedule or follow up on home inspection services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## How Your Information Is Processed

Our website uses [Trashbox](https://trashbox.io/), our web development provider, to securely deliver contact form submissions. When you submit the form, your information is transmitted through Trashbox's systems and forwarded to us by email notification. Trashbox processes this data solely to operate the contact form on our behalf and does not sell your personal information.

## Data Retention

We keep contact form submissions only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and maintain reasonable business records related to our services.

## Your Choices

You may choose not to submit the contact form. If you have already contacted us and would like to ask about information you submitted, email us at [txcovenanthomeinspections@gmail.com](mailto:txcovenanthomeinspections@gmail.com).

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date shown on this page will reflect any changes.
