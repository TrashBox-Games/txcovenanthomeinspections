Hi, I'm Joel Piercy. I grew up in the North Houston area graduating from high school in New Caney and later earned my bachelor's degree from Sam Houston State University.

For the past seven years, I've served as the Young Adults Pastor at New Life Church in Kingwood. It has been an honor to invest in the lives of youth and young adults throughout the Kingwood community.

In recent years, I completed the Champions School of Real Estate program and earned my Texas Real Estate Commission Professional Home Inspector license. Since then, I've had the opportunity to help homebuyers and homeowners gain confidence in one of life's biggest investments through thorough and professional home inspections.

My main mission is to serve you and your family, not just by being a service, but by becoming your home inspector for life. I look forward to earning your trust and getting to know you as your home inspector.
